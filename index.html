What We Have Done

ATI MEDITOP

ATI MEDiTOP is a complete Hospital Information System which facilitates Management, Clinicians, Technologists, Nurses, Technicians, Support Staffs & Patients of a Hospital.

ATI EMR

The Electronic Medical Record (EMR) contains longitudinal health information about individual patients. It includes a range of data in a comprehensive form.

ATI LIS

ATI’s Laboratory Information System (LIS) releases a new window for the laboratory in Robustness, Modality, Security, Cost Reduction, and Administrative Conducts.

ATI PACS

ATI’s Picture Archiving and Communication System (PACS) is a web-based system that increases the efficiency and maximizes the profit of an imaging center.

ATI ERP

ATI ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) software is an integrated system used for businesses to combine, organize, and maintain operational data.

ATI MBS

ATI Mobile Billing System provides a simple, secure, and reliable platform for collecting payments via a consumer’s mobile phone.
